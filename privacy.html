Privacy Policy

At Faddy Insight Mortgages, your privacy matters. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data you submit via our contact form or email link.

1. Data Collection

When you email us at fm@faddyinsight.com, we collect your name, email address, and any other details you include in your message.

2. Use of Data

We use your contact details solely to respond to your mortgage or credit enquiry. We do not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

3. Data Retention

We retain your email and correspondence for up to 2 years for record-keeping and compliance with Central Bank regulations. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

4. Your Rights

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of any personal data we hold.
  • Right to rectification: Request corrections if your data is inaccurate.
  • Right to erasure: Ask us to delete your data from our records.

5. Contact

For any privacy concerns, email fm@faddyinsight.com.
